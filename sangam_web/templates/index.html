The Divine City of Ujjain

One of the seven sacred cities of India, where ancient spirituality meets divine energy

A Sacred Heritage Spanning Millennia

Ujjain, known as Ujjayini in ancient times, stands as one of India's most revered pilgrimage destinations. This sacred city on the banks of the holy Shipra River has been a center of spiritual learning and divine worship for over 2,000 years.

Home to the magnificent Mahakaleshwar Jyotirlinga, Ujjain is where Lord Shiva resides in his most powerful form. The city's spiritual significance is further enhanced by its role as one of the four sacred sites of the Kumbh Mela, where drops of the nectar of immortality are believed to have fallen.
